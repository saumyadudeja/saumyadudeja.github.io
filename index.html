Welcome!

I just graduated from college and have been looking for a Full time job as a Web Developer at a socially responsible company. I was 6 when my dad introduced me to the internet, and I have been deeply intrigued by the web ever since. I know that Tech is not the friendliest place in the world right now (courtesy: 'The Social Dilemma'), but we constitute this community and we need to make Tech friendlier, kinder and a better place to be in. The best thing about the internet is that I can make my voice heard, even if I am a 'common person'. If we do things right, we can make our voices heard and make a tangible impact, even if it's just in the community. Things that get me excited are technology, health, plants and food. Something that gets my heart raging is climate change. It breaks my heart how little things such as our shopping habits affect the Earth. I am here to be a part of the second generation of Tech, which is more inclusive and open. This is my portfolio website, which is always a work in progress.
