Survey Web App

For the Master's thesis project, I built an in-house survey tool to understand mobility preferences of citizens of the most polluted cities in Germany. I started with domain modeling and the wireframes and on to building the web app. After this, I worked on the view logic and finally the templates and test cases. This has been a great learning experience for me because when I was starting out, I had no experience with Django and building this web app gave a boost to my confidence towards becoming a Full Stack Developer.
